05 — About

The Story

Software Engineer · iOS Developer · Full Stack Web Dev · AI Native

I'm a New York-based creative technologist who treats code as a medium for storytelling. I started building iOS apps nearly a decade ago, and that foundation in native development shaped how I think about interfaces — performance-first, user-obsessed, detail-oriented.

Today I build AI-native tools and full-stack applications that bridge technical architecture with creative vision. I rebuilt an AI triage system from 60% to 93% accuracy and presented the results to a CEO. I built MotionFrame — a browser-based text-to-video pipeline with no backend. I direct AI-generated video with Google Gemini and Veo 3. I ship fast, I ship clean, and I build things because they need to exist.

I'm an AI-Native Fellow at Pursuit, where I've also served as an instructor — because I believe the best way to learn is to teach. My creative philosophy is simple: AI doesn't replace creative instinct. It raises the stakes for having one.
